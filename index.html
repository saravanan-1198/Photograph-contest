RHS Photographic Competition


Are you obsessed with taking snapshots of your garden? Enter this flower-photo-friendly contest, which includes categories for 11-17-year-olds and even kids under 11 years old. This photo competition is organized by the Royal Horticulture Society, which is targets the photographers, horticulturists, and garden lovers. The categories for this nature photography contest are celebrating gardens, welcoming garden wildlife, abstract, urban gardening, etc.

Entry Fee: Free Prize: £5,000 (approximately $6,345) for the RHS photo competition. The young winner of the RHS photo competition gets £750. There are other categories which have prizes from £150 to £500. Submission

Dates: Till 10am of 31 January, 2020. These dates may change each year.
